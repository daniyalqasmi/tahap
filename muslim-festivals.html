Muslim Festivals

2 festivals found

Eid al-Fitr

End of Ramadan (Islamic calendar)

Festival marking the end of fasting during Ramadan. A day of joy, prayer, and charity.

Eid al-Adha

During Hajj, Islamic lunar calendar (Dhul Hijjah 10th)

Commemorates Prophet Ibrahim’s (Abraham’s) willingness to sacrifice his son for Allah. Traditions: Animal sacrifice, family gatherings, sharing food with the poor.

Ramadan

(Month-long)

Holy month of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community.
